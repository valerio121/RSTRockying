            &lt;h2&gt;
                When Does The Contest Start?&lt;/h2&gt;
            &lt;p&gt;
                The Contest begins on the 8th of December 2012 and ends on the 20th of January 2013.&lt;/p&gt;
            &lt;h2&gt;
                Tell Me More...&lt;/h2&gt;
            &lt;ul&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Every week we post a photo and all you have to do is write a funny, entertaining
                    caption for it.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;The contest runs for a total of six weeks and every week there will be a new photo.
                    So, six weeks, six photos, six cash prizes to be won.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;At the end of every week, we chose the best caption for the photo and the winner
                    for each week gets a cash prize of 500 INR (Indian Rupees). The winners will be
                    notified by an update on the page. &lt;/li&gt;
            &lt;/ul&gt;
            &lt;h2&gt;
                How Can I Participate?&lt;/h2&gt;
            &lt;ul&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Like our facebook page &lt;a href="http://www.facebook.com/RockyingMag" target="_blank"&gt;
                    here&lt;/a&gt;.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Stay tuned for our updates on the contest on facebook.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;We release a photo on facebook every week. Just comment your craziest and funniest
                    caption for the photo. &lt;/li&gt;
            &lt;/ul&gt;
            &lt;h2&gt;
                Are There Any Rules Too?&lt;/h2&gt;
            &lt;ol&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;To participate, you must be a &lt;a href="http://www.facebook.com/RockyingMag" target"_blank"&gt;
                Rockying&lt;/a&gt; fan on facebook.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Duplication of captions is strictly prohibited. If you are found duplicating a comment,
                    you will be disqualified immediately.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Participants are not allowed to comment more than once.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;The winner will not be decided on the basis of "facebook likes".&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Comments on the Rockying page post will only qualify for the contest. No comments
                    on shared posts will be considered.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Decision of the Rockying team will be final in case of any controversy.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Even the best caption will be disqualified in case we find the participant violating
                    the rules.&lt;/li&gt;
            &lt;/ol&gt;
            &lt;h2&gt;
                Commenting Guidelines.
            &lt;/h2&gt;
            &lt;ul&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;We reserve the right to remove any content that might be found extremely offensive
                    or threatening. So, please watch your language and respect other people's views,
                    beliefs and emotions. &lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;The comments you write should be either in English or Hindi languages. Comments
                    in any other language are not eligible for the contest. &lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Please donâ€™t engage in personal attacks (on admins, other commenters or any individual),
                    persistent trolling or mindless abuse.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Hate speech: Racism, sexism and homophobia will not be tolerated.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;No Spam: Spam and advertising content will be removed. &lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Relevancy: Please keep conversations relevant. Off-topic comments are subject to
                    removal in order to keep the thread on track.&lt;/li&gt;
            &lt;/ul&gt;
            &lt;h2&gt;
                Did I win?
            &lt;/h2&gt;
            &lt;ul&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;We will chose the best caption for every photo and notify the winner by an update
                    on our facebook page. &lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;If you win, you will be asked to give your e-mail id and other account details to
                    receive the cash prize. &lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Any conversation regarding the prize money should be done either through mails or
                    messaging on the page.&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Winners will receive their prize through NEFT (National Electronics Funds Transfer).
                    &lt;b&gt;So, it is mandatory for the participants to have an online account.&lt;/b&gt;&lt;/li&gt;
                &lt;li&gt;Remember ! You must claim your prize money within a week from the day of announcement.
                    After which we are not liable to pay you the winning amount. So, what are you waiting
                    for? &lt;/li&gt;
            &lt;/ul&gt;

                Be Crazy, Be Creative and Get Winning !&lt;/h3&gt;
