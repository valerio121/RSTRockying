&lt;h3&gt;4. &lt;i&gt;Lapak Jhapak Tu Aa Re Badarwa &lt;/i&gt;(Boot Polish) &lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;hr&gt;
&lt;a href="https://www.youtube.com/embed/yDr9g_x2nYk" frameborder="0" allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen" class="youtube"&gt;&lt;img src="http://www.rockying.com/art/bollywood/Most-Funny-Songs/Lapak-Jhapak-Tu-Aa-Re-Badarwa.png" alt="Click to Watch Lapak-Jhapak Tu aa re Badarwa" title="Lapak-Jhapak Tu aa re Badarwa"&gt;&lt;/a&gt;
&lt;hr&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
The old bald man, played by David, is in the midst of his inmates (most of them baldies! Notice the heads in the image above? Click it to watch the song here! ). Just a moment ago, a professor among those jail-birds- (who was convicted for trying to sell a fraudulent hair-growth formula! )- had put forth his profound ‘philosophy’ about ‘scientific’ connection of &lt;i&gt;‘Ghatayen’&lt;/i&gt;(Dark rain-clouds) and &lt;i&gt;‘Jataen’&lt;/i&gt; (Thick hair) and David is desperately trying out the musical formula based on Tansen's &lt;i&gt;Raag Malhaar&lt;/i&gt;, in order to get hair on all their bald scalps! Sung by the great Manna Dey, this song is a musical-comedic gem. 
&lt;/p&gt;
