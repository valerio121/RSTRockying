A spoof is nothing but a parody sometimes done for comedy and sometimes (mostly, these days) for sarcasm. And if you are aware of the genre, you know how hilarious it is. Right? You get to watch the funniest versions of the most popular songs, movies, even sitcoms. Becoming one of the most popular and sought-after genre for independent film-makers, it would be a shame to not talk about them here. In this edition of spoofy entertainment, we're covering songs best spoofed by none other than the internet. So, let's get down to watching all of the spoofy musical drama produced by web film-makers in India. This is going to be so much fun. Le'go!
