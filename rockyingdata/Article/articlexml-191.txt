                    Origin: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India&lt;br /&gt;
                &lt;/h4&gt;
                &lt;br /&gt;
                &lt;p&gt;

                    and CFO of the second largest food and beverage business, PepsiCo. Her real strong
                    attitude has helped the company garner $30 billion in couple of years. She has a
                    Master degree in public management from the Yale University and also a Master degree
                    in Finance and Marketing from IIM, Kolkata. She has also been awarded the prestigious
                    Padma Bhushan for her outstanding efforts in commerce and for truly being an inspiration
                    for the nation.

                    and also serves as a Global Advisor at the Harvard business school and also provides
                    her services as a Non executive director at Nestle SA. Her hard work and dedication
                    has always been rewarded and respected, she received the Padma Shri award for her
                    contributions in the segment of Trade and Industry.

                &lt;h3&gt;
                    Kiran Mazumdar Shaw&lt;/h3&gt;
                &lt;h4&gt;
                    Origin: Banglore, Karnataka, India
                    &lt;br /&gt;
                &lt;/h4&gt;
                &lt;br /&gt;
                &lt;p&gt;
                    Facing various difficulties and challenges because of her youth, charm, gender and
                    her untested business model, Kiran always dreamt of achieving big and she made her
                    dream true. A proud Bangalorean and the Chairperson of Biocon, she built her bio
                    pharmaceutical company with a well balanced business portfolio and made herself
                    worth $900 million. She has always inspired the younger generation with the challenge
                    she took in the field of Biochemical’s. She has received various prestigious awards
                    for her outstanding efforts that inspires the younger generation and it also makes
                    the country proud.

                    Coleman &amp; Co. Ltd which owns the most popular newspaper daily- Times of India and
                    other such large newspapers. She is one of the most powerful and influential women

                    cyclones, earthquakes and epidemics. Indu Jain's championing of women's causes is

                    of women entrepreneurship.

                &lt;p&gt;
                    With her specialized degree in economics, Priya entered her family business and
                    worked under her father as a marketing manager at the Park Hotel in Delhi. After
                    the death of Surrendra Paul, she succeeded him in 1990 as a chairperson and since
                    then she did not have to look back. Her contributions towards the Hospitality industry

                    hospitality sector.

                &lt;/h4&gt;

                &lt;p&gt;
                    Balaji Telefilms and their TV serials are watched by most of us every day. She sits
                    as the creative head on the board of Balaji Telefilms and has many 'hits' in bag.
                    Her most famous tele serials are ‘Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi’, ‘Kahani Ghar
                    Ghar Ki’ and many others, making her the Queen Bee of the Indian soap opera scene.
                    She has won the Hall of Fame award at the 6th Indian Tele Awards during 2006 for
                    her extraordinary contribution to the Indian television industry. She has been highly
                    admired for her entrepreneurial skills.

                &lt;br /&gt;
                &lt;p&gt;

                    Kinetic Finance. She is solely responsible for the transformation from a moped company
                    to a manufacturer of complete versatility. She has developed and designed the marketing
                    strategies for Kinetic and helped it grow vastly over these years. She has been
                    honored “The Face of the Millennium” by India Today for her enthusiastic business
                    approach and she has also been selected as “The Global Leader of Tomorrow” by the
                    world economic forum.
