  <Text>&lt;p&gt;
    A password getting hacked is the biggest threat for online data storage and privacy.&lt;img
        src="http://rudrasofttech.com/rockying/art/internet/Password-Protection/gmail-password-hacked.jpg"
        title="Password Hacked" alt="" style="width: 300px;" class="pull-left" /&gt;
    Every individual’s account gets compromised at least once in their lifetime. And
    the repercussions do not present a very pretty picture.
&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    Almost 550, 000 user passwords on Yahoo are hacked in an annual basis, 6 million
    LinkedIn accounts are hacked and Facebook account-hacking figures are unbelievably
    staggering.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    What should one do to avoid such security breaches?&amp;nbsp;&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    Read on. The tips are simple and just demand a little caution during your online
    presence.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h4&gt;
    DOs:&lt;/h4&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;p&gt;
            Always keep passwords that have &lt;b&gt;special characters&lt;/b&gt; such as @, *, # and comprise
            of at least two to three numbers. For example, AB*12#@ would be a difficult password
            to hack than simple ones such as XYZ123. While deciding on a password, online profiles
            do highlight password strength indicator. Do follow that!&lt;/p&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;p&gt;
            Ensure that you &lt;b&gt;change your passwords&lt;/b&gt; every two months. If you are dealing
            with high-profile or critical data, modifying passwords every month is advisable.&lt;/p&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;p&gt;
            &amp;nbsp;Buy &lt;b&gt;total internet security anti-viruses&lt;/b&gt; and keep them up-to-date.
            Malwares also steal passwords.&lt;/p&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;h4&gt;
    DON'Ts:&lt;/h4&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;p&gt;
            &lt;b&gt;Never close &lt;/b&gt;the web page from the close button that is situated next to minimize
            and maximize button. Always Log Off from your account before you close the page
            or shut your laptop down. Direct closing of pages can leave passwords saved in the
            server and lead to anyone stealing them.&lt;/p&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;p&gt;
            &lt;b&gt;Do not keep the same password&lt;/b&gt; for different social and official accounts
            you hold online. This is a gross mistake and hackers find it extremely easy to hack
            such accounts. For easy remembering, remember a pattern in your mind and choose
            passwords accordingly. For example, flower name sequences,country sequences etc.
            can help you remember passwords at ease. The strategy should be easy to remember
            and hard to speculate!&lt;/p&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;p&gt;
            &lt;b&gt;Avoid using public computers&lt;/b&gt; or working on anyone’s else’s PC. If compulsion
            is the call of the time, then do not save passwords and ask if the network or the
            WI-fi connection is secure enough or not. Hackers always seize or capture password
            images in unsecured networks.&lt;/p&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;p&gt;
            Bible rule: &lt;b&gt;Never share your passwords&lt;/b&gt;. You would be digging your own pit!&lt;/p&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
