  <Text>&lt;p&gt;
    One of India’s most visited places and an absolute must-see for every traveler,
    Ladakh is an intriguing and bewitching “moonscape” of craggy hills, soft snow and
    surprising sand dunes. With jaw dropping sights and people who are remarkably warm
    and happy, it draws you back again year after year. It is a formidably large place
    though, 11700 sq km in all, and we are here to guide you through it all. Here are
    the five must-do things when you find yourself happily lost in Ladakh.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;
    Top Spot: The Lakes&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/pangong-so-village.jpg"
        alt="Pangong So Village" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/melting_pangong_tso.jpg"
        alt="Melting Pangong Tso" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/pangong-tso.jpg"
        alt="Pangong Tso" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    Ladakh is home to some of the most spectacular lakes on earth. There is the phenomenal
    Pangong-tso Lake, the highest salt water lake in the world and the most famous of
    all Ladakh sights which undoubtedly made your eyes pop out in the final scenes of
    3 Idiots. Almost equal in beauty is Tso –Moriri, a remote and beautiful lake that
    is a 7 hour drive away from Leh. Not as well known as these two heavyweights, but
    unique in its own way, is the Lobame Tso, a volcanic lake formed by a meteor crash
    in Pulthang’s surreal soda-ash plains.
&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;
    Close Second: Nubra Valley&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/road_across_nubra_valley.jpg"
        alt="Road Across Nubra Valley" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/Nubra-valley-in-leh.jpg"
        alt="Nubra Valley In Leh" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    Is there any other place in the world that combines a sandy desert with snow, mountains,
    twin humped camels, an azure river and a ride across one of the highest roads in
    the world?&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/khardung-la-pass.jpg"
        alt="Khardung La Pass" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    The valley of the river Nubra, that lies on the other side of the Khardung-La pass
    (a full 18380 feet high) does all of these in perfect harmony. Nubra valley is a
    shutterbug’s delight with some of the most amazing natural vistas to be found in
    India.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;
    Tricky Third: The Ride&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/the_road.jpg"
        alt="Road To Leh" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    The best way to reach Leh? Ride in with the mountain winds on the spectacular Manali-
    Leh road.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/sarchu.jpg"
        alt="Sarchu" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    This almost 500 km long road is the dream ride of every Royal Enfield owner in the
    country and passes through some amazing scenery as it winds its way through Rohtang,
    Koksar, Tandi and Sarchu. Keep your flight tickets away and push down on the throttle,
    the ride to Leh is sure to be one of the most memorable trips of your lifetimes.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;
    Fabulous Fourth: Leh&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/leh.jpg"
        alt="Leh City" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    The capital of Ladakh is not only a base for your exploration of the far flung areas
    of the district; it is also a destination in itself. A throbbing city that is the
    heart of Ladakhi culture, Leh is a place of great beauty &amp; joy in the summers.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/leh-culture.jpg"
        alt="Prayer Wheels, Buddhist Monks, Stupa and Prayer Flags in Leh" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/shanti_stupa.jpg"
        alt="" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/Shanti-Stupa.jpg"
        alt="" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    Landmarks such as the Shanti Stupa , which offers an unparalleled view across the
    valley and the Leh Royal Palace that watches over the city, are must visits when
    in Leh. Stay for an extended period of time and you will also find a food &amp; music
    culture that is delightfully surprising.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;h3&gt;
    Last But Not the Least: Zanskar&lt;/h3&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/zanskar-valley.jpg"
        alt="Zanskar Valley" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/zanskar-from-Ladakh.jpg"
        alt="Zanskar Valley From Ladakh" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/zanskar.jpg"
        alt="Zanskar" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/conflence_zanskar-indus.jpg"
        alt="Confluence of Indus River and Zanskar" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    In the lap of the Zanskar mountain range, river Indus and Zanskar meet at this stunningly
    gorgeous cosmos.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/Chadar-zanskar-trek.jpg"
        alt="The Chadarr Track, Zanskar" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    This one is only for the strong willed. The wild Zanskar River gets frozen over
    in the harsh Ladakhi winters and turns into a hiking trail for the brave and fearless.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    An experience that cannot be found anywhere else in the country, this famed “Chadar
    Trek” is now the stuff of legends and if you are bold enough to go to Ladakh in
    peak winters, it is sure to give you bragging rights for the rest of your life.&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;div style="text-align:center;"&gt;
    &lt;img style="max-width:100%;" src="//rockying.com/art/Travel/Lost-in-Ladakh/leh-ladakh-night-and-day.jpg"
        alt="Concurrent Night and Day in Leh-Ladakh" /&gt;
&lt;/div&gt;
&lt;h4&gt;
    Be in Ladakh, be on top of the world!&lt;/h4&gt;
