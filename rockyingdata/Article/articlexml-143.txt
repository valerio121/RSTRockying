            &lt;h5&gt;
                There’s nothing easier than waking up in the morning, throwing on your favorite
                graphic tee, shades with bag in hand, and heading out the door. The graphic tee
                is comfort’s coolest friend. We all have at least two over-sized, off the shoulder,
                slouchy tees that year after year seem to creep back into the front of our wardrobe.&lt;/h5&gt;
            &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Graphic%20Tees/New%20Picture%20%2823%29.png"
                title="A Cool Human Print Graphic Tee" alt="" style="height: 600px; width: 481px;" /&gt;&lt;h5&gt;
                    Check out a few of our favorites and while we’re at it, why not learn how to make
                    a graphic tee on your own. It’s the perfect way to spend a rainy summer day (or
                    any other day for that matter).
                &lt;/h5&gt;
        &lt;/div&gt;
        &lt;div class="slide" style="text-align: center;"&gt;
            &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Graphic%20Tees/New%20Picture%20%2824%29.png"
                title="Graphic Tee" alt="" style="height: 600px; width: 362px;" /&gt;&lt;h5&gt;
                    Making your own graphic tee is easy as 1, 2, 3. Literally. Follow these three simple
                    steps, and you could have enough graphic tees for each day of the week.
                &lt;/h5&gt;
        &lt;/div&gt;
        &lt;div class="slide" style="text-align: center;"&gt;
            &lt;h5&gt;
                STEP ONE&lt;/h5&gt;
            &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Graphic%20Tees/New%20Picture%20%2825%29.png"
                title="Plain Black Tees" alt="" style="height: 600px; width: 467px;"&gt;
            &lt;p&gt;
                Buy a plain colored T-shirt anywhere from any of the markets. V-neck, crew-neck,
                crop top—your choice. (Washing the tee first allows for a more vintage feel)
            &lt;/p&gt;
        &lt;/div&gt;
        &lt;div class="slide" style="text-align: center;"&gt;
            &lt;h5&gt;
                STEP TWO&lt;/h5&gt;
            &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Graphic%20Tees/New%20Picture%20%2826%29.png"
                title="Your Favorite Photo On Your Tee" alt="" style="height: 600px; width: 480px;"&gt;&lt;p&gt;
                    Pick out your favorite photo and print your picks with Iron-on Computer Paper. The
                    possibilities are endless! Find the paper from your nearest Arts &amp;amp; Craft store.
                &lt;/p&gt;
        &lt;/div&gt;
        &lt;div class="slide" style="text-align: center;"&gt;
