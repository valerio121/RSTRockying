                    2006, was Huang Guangyu. Country Garden's initial offering raised about $1.6 billion,
                    or as much as Google raised in 2004 in the United States. Yang's net worth was estimated
                    at $16.2 billion USD in October 2007. Yang is a 2003 graduate of The Ohio State
