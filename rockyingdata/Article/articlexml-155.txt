  <Text>&lt;p&gt;
    This week it is all about men’s fashion. With a very limited set of accessories
    a man’s wardrobe can offer, here are a few corporate must-haves:&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    &lt;br&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;h3&gt;
            UNDERSTANDING CUFFLINKS:&lt;/h3&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Fashion-For-Men/cufflinks.jpg"
        title="Cufflinks" alt="" style="width: 700px;"&gt;&lt;br&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;
    &amp;nbsp;A sexy and a suave cuff link can add unprecedented charm to a French sleeve
    or any formal gathering. Men who are up for the corporate setup cannot afford to
    look casual or flamboyant for that matter. All they can experiment with is detailing
    making some individual fashion statements. Be it French cuffs, torpedo cufflinks,
    chain cufflinks or silk knots, these are the cleverest and the smartest accessories
    any man can have. &lt;a href="http://everyguyed.com/howto/buy-wear-cufflinks/"&gt;Read more&lt;/a&gt;
    about cufflinks and its variety.
    &lt;br&gt;
&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;h3&gt;
            NECK-TIES:&amp;nbsp;
            &lt;br&gt;
        &lt;/h3&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Fashion-For-Men/Neck.Ties.jpg"
        title="Neck-Ties" alt="" style="width: 700px;"&gt;&lt;br&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;
    If you are a fresher to the corporate setup, a thin black tie is a strong recommendation
    for your workstation. Wear it to work and loosen it up for the party later, a black
    tie will definitely make corporate dressing more fun! Apart from that, invest in
    blue ties, specifically striped ones. As a rule, men avoid patterns and an interesting
    match of colors. The corporates won’t kill you if you occasionally flirt with colors.
    Will they?&lt;br&gt;
    &lt;br&gt;
&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;h3&gt;
            BELTS:
        &lt;/h3&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Fashion-For-Men/Belts-For-men.jpg"
        title="Belts" alt="" style="width: 700px;"&gt;&lt;br&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;
&lt;blockquote&gt;
    A belt is the perfect culmination of your look. A black and a brown leather belt
    (buy those reversible ones) should never be missing from your wardrobe. A belt no
    more is just meant to hold up your pants from slipping! It is totally subtle on
    style and a real eye-catcher.&lt;br&gt;
    &lt;br&gt;
&lt;/blockquote&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;h3&gt;
            OFFICE BAGS:&lt;/h3&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Fashion-For-Men/Bags.jpg"
        title="Office Bags" alt="" style="width: 700px;"&gt;&lt;br&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;
&amp;nbsp;It is time for some change. Do away with boring backpacks and invest in some
leather laptop cases and bags. It is totally upswing and speaks volumes about one’s
classic tastes. Tan brown leather or chequered black leather bags with smart buckles
will inspire your everyday corporate wardrobe. Here’s a tip match your bags with
your shoes and belts. An office bag is worth an investment and is something that
combines style and functionality at ease!&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;h3&gt;
            JACKETS &amp;amp; SUITS:&lt;/h3&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Fashion-For-Men/Jackets.N.Suits.jpg"
        title="Jackets-n-Suits" alt="" style="width: 700px;"&gt;&lt;br&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;
&amp;nbsp;Keep a few light blue and beige linen jackets for a lousy Friday at work.
Team it up with brown trousers and spruce up that boring image of yours into a fashion
avatar without much ado! For daily wear suits, a black suit is an essential apart
from a mute blue suit. Make sure the fabrics you choose for your suits are not flashy,
dressy and go well with all shirts.&lt;br&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;h3&gt;
            CARD HOLDERS:&amp;nbsp;
        &lt;/h3&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
&lt;p&gt;
    &lt;img src="http://rockying.com/art/Lifestyle/Fashion/Fashion-For-Men/card.Holders.jpg"
        title="Card Holders" alt="" style="width: 700px;"&gt;&lt;br&gt;
&lt;/p&gt;
Well, Mont Blanc card holders are way too materialistic for men and usually they
go out of their way to possess one of those uniquely hand-crafted beauties. If that
does not suit your budget, a leather card holder will boost your confidence manifold
each time you reach out for it to pull your card out!&lt;br&gt;
&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;Well….. &lt;b&gt;this&lt;/b&gt; will probably be one of the most important fashion &lt;b&gt;accessory&lt;/b&gt;
        for any man, hands down!&lt;/li&gt;&lt;/ul&gt;

&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;&lt;b&gt;TO SUM IT ALL UP&lt;/b&gt;: Here is the Before-After look. Find out what you lack and
